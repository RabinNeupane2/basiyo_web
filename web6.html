Atithi Resort and Spa is an unique resort seated at the heart of the city of lakes, Pokhara. ‘Atithi’ is ‘Guest’ in English typically chosen to reflect the well nurtured Nepalese culture of revering ‘atithi-guest’ as cites the verse in Sanskrit ‘अतिथि देवो भवः (Guest is God). The eternal intimacy of Mount Fishtail and Fewa lake has been the source of inspiration to serve our most valued guests together with the pristine beauty of Pokhara and its exquisite surrounding.
